Purpose of Colors, Target Audience and Fetaures Used

Dark Blue: Conveys professionalism, trust, and stability, creating a strong foundational color that resonates with a serious and academic tone.

Gradient Blue: Adds a modern and dynamic feel to the site, suggesting creativity and depth while maintaining a calming aesthetic.

White: Provides contrast, enhancing readability and giving a clean, fresh look that emphasizes content without overwhelming the viewer.

Target Audience:

The site targets students, professionals, and anyone interested in personal development and growth. The professional color scheme and layout appeal to an academic audience, while the engaging visuals and themes of perseverance and character development attract younger individuals looking for inspiration and guidance.
